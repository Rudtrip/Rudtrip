## About

> Product Manager with three years of experience delivering education, SaaS, CRM, automation, and AI-enabled products.

I lead product initiatives end-to-end: discover user and business needs, structure requirements, prioritize the backlog, coordinate design and engineering, validate functionality, and move solutions into production.

My background combines product management with practical technical depth. I work confidently with software architecture discussions, APIs, databases, authentication, AI workflows, CRM integrations, quality assurance, and full-stack product delivery. This allows me to translate business goals into requirements engineers can implement and stakeholders can evaluate.

At Reezonly, I have delivered more than 15 major LMS features and modules for over 12,000 learners and education professionals. I coordinate a cross-functional team of five specialists and focus on predictable delivery, user experience, process automation, and measurable product outcomes.
